About Me

Hi, I'm Tin, a passionate Game developer and AI researcher based in Da Nang.

I started my career as an R&D AI Engineer at Atoma Technology, where I developed solutions for 3D body scanning, virtual clothing fitting, fire detection, and automated parking systems. This experience laid the foundation for my interest in combining AI with interactive applications.

I later transitioned to game development at Gianty Vietnam, where I've been working on multiplayer systems, gameplay mechanics, and AI-powered tools to accelerate game development workflows.

My career path follows a progression from Roblox to UEFN to Unreal Engine, with increasing technical complexity at each step. I'm particularly interested in UEFN's modular code organization approach and applying similar principles to Unreal Engine development.
